<p class="t-lead">LearnUXD brings you this course on current trends in user experience design and tackle some additional project work to experiment in new and emerging areas.</p>

By the end of this course students will be able to:

- Recall and explain current trends in interactive design and related fields and apply them to real-world projects.
- Develop a value for stewardship of their time and talents and of lifelong learning.
- Research additional knowledge and skills on their own to reach fluency with terms and skills.
- Create an experimental interactive project.
